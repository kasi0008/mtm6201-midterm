We are on a plant based mission

The coconuts we source are non-gmo certified and is pure milk
with no water added or made from a concentrate. Coconut milk provides
healthy essential fat, nutrients and the creamy texture to our ice cream.
Coconut milk is very infusible because of it's rich fatty quality and
therefore we are able to produce a full flavor experience without any of
the fake coconut flavor.

Bringing more plants to the table is not only good for you, but good for
the planet. Eating plants requires fewer resources, from energy to water,
than a standard meat-heavy diet. We are a 100% vegan company.
